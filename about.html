About / Law enforcement, military, enthusiasts

Machinists first. Shooters always.

Accurate-Mag Products is the world's largest producer of precision bolt action and sniper rifle components. Every part is held to military grade specifications and made entirely in America.

The company

A division of B.M.L. Tool & Manufacturing

Accurate-Mag Products is a division of the B.M.L. Tool and Manufacturing Corporation in Monroe, Connecticut. B.M.L. Tool is a veteran owned small business with avid gun owners and shooters populating its workforce.

Because B.M.L. Tool was a premier tool and die shop before it joined the firearm industry, the natural entry point was the metal formed magazine. What began as a few magazine types has grown into a catalog of more than 40 magazine styles and calibers, plus bottom metal, chassis systems, and complete rifles.

Most of that work ships quietly inside OEM components of Savage, Remington, Ruger, and Colt rifles. If you shoot precision bolt guns, you have probably already experienced the quality of an Accurate-Mag part without knowing it.

Now in its third generation of family ownership, the corporation's officers include Anthony R. Battaglia, who represented the company in Senator Chris Murphy's 2017 Connecticut manufacturer spotlight.

How the line grew

From forming dies to complete rifles

Each expansion followed the last as a direct engineering progression.

Foundation / 1968

A two-car garage in the Bridgeport area

Philip Battaglia and two partners found B.M.L. Tool in 1968, building its reputation in precision tooling and metal forming, the exact disciplines behind a reliable magazine body.

Entry

The metal formed magazine

Accurate-Mag enters the firearms industry through metal formed magazines, launching with a handful of types built to service configurations.

Expansion

40+ styles and calibers

The magazine line grows to over 40 styles and calibers spanning short action, long action, and specialty formats.

Machining

CNC and bottom metal

A new CNC area comes online, enabling bottom metal kits for Remington and Savage rifles that pair directly with the magazines already in production.

Systems

Modular chassis design

As precision rifles move from wood and fiberglass stocks to machined modular chassis, Accurate-Mag designs a chassis system that improves accuracy, modularity, and function on existing bolt actions.

Complete rifles

Purpose-built rifle systems

Rather than repackaging stock actions, Accurate-Mag works with Savage to create the first .300 and .338 Norma Magnum production rifle, and pairs Remington 700 models with barreled actions custom made by GA Precision.

Who we serve

Law enforcement. Military. Enthusiasts.

The same components run in service rifles, agency builds, and weekend match guns. One standard for all three.
