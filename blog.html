Ultralite chassis arrives for the Ruger 10/22

After years of requests for a 10/22 chassis, the Ultralite line proved the right fit for the action. It carries M-LOK slots, an ARCA rail, a barricade stop, and thumb rests, and accepts standard AR pistol grips and carbine type stocks.

Product news / Chassis

Savage Axis joins the Ultralite lineup

The Savage Axis becomes a supported action in the Ultralite family, with M-LOK along the forend sides and bottom, an integral ARCA rail, barricade stop, and thumb rests. Compatible with AR-15 grips and stocks, including rifle style stocks.

See the chassis line
SHOT Show / Jan 2024

Sport-Tact Ultra Lite debuts in Las Vegas

Introduced for Remington 700 short actions, the Ultra Lite is a single-piece chassis with an ARCA rail front end, integrated QD cups, and thumb shelves. A 22 oz base weight brings chassis ergonomics to light hunting and sporting builds.
