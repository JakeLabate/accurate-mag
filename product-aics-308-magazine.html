Configuration

Choose the right build

Capacity

Available in 5 round and 10 round bodies. Both share the same width and length footprint and fit any AICS type bottom metal or chassis system.

Blinder plate

With the blinder plate, the magazine handles regular length cartridges to 2.875" OAL. Without it, extended length cartridges to 2.950" OAL fit at the cost of some rigidity, a favorite of handloaders chasing high BC bullets.

Cartridge family

The full .308 family runs in this magazine, including .243 Win and .260 Rem, so one body covers multiple barrel configurations.

FAQ

Fitment and configuration questions

Will this magazine fit my chassis or bottom metal?

Yes, this magazine fits any AICS pattern bottom metal or chassis system, including short action AICS footprints from major chassis manufacturers.

Which calibers run in the .308 body?

The entire .308 Winchester family of cartridges runs in this magazine, including .243 Winchester and .260 Remington.

What does the blinder plate option change?

With the blinder plate, the magazine feeds regular length cartridges to a 2.875 inch maximum overall length. Without the blinder plate, extended length cartridges to 2.950 inches fit, with some reduction in magazine rigidity.

Who uses this magazine as OEM equipment?

The .308/7.62x51mm AM box magazine has been the OEM choice of manufacturers including Remington Arms, Savage Arms, and McMillan Rifles, and Ruger selected it as the OEM magazine for the Gunsite Scout rifle.
